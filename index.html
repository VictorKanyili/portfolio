Who I Am

About Me

I'm a Cybersecurity Analyst at Peer Carbon Earth, Nairobi, Kenya, where I work at a unique intersection of climate technology and information security — building secure Azure cloud architectures and applying cryptographic integrity mechanisms to carbon credit verification workflows.

My day-to-day spans Azure RBAC & Microsoft Entra ID administration, threat detection through a Wazuh + Splunk dual-SIEM pipeline, incident response playbook development, and Python-driven security automation. I map all detection efforts to MITRE ATT&CK for structured threat intelligence.

I hold a First Class Honours degree in Information Security & Forensics and am actively working toward the AZ-500 (Azure Security Engineer) and eJPT (penetration testing) certifications to deepen both my defensive and offensive security expertise.

Outside the terminal — I enjoy public speaking, hiking around Nairobi, graphic design, and tracking financial markets. I'm open to both onsite and remote security roles.
